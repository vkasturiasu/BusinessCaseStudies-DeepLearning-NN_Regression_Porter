## Context

**Porter** is India's largest marketplace for intra-city logistics and a leader in the country's $40 billion intra-city logistics market. The company is committed to improving the lives of 150,000+ driver-partners by providing consistent earning opportunities and independence. To date, Porter has serviced over 5 million customers.

### Business Challenge

Porter partners with numerous restaurants to deliver food directly to customers. With a diverse pool of delivery partners available, the company needs to provide customers with accurate estimated delivery times based on:
- What they are ordering
- Pickup location
- Delivery destination
- Available delivery partners

### Solution Approach

This dataset contains the necessary features to train a regression model for delivery time estimation, enabling Porter to offer reliable delivery time predictions to customers.
